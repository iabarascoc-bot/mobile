Voter Registration Requirements

  • Must be a Filipino citizen (by birth or naturalization).
  • At least 18 years old on election day.
  • Must have lived in the Philippines for at least 1 year.
  • Must have lived in your city/municipality for at least 6 months.
  • Bring at least one valid ID (National ID, Passport, Driver’s License, etc.).
  • School ID may be accepted depending on COMELEC office rules.
  • Must appear in person for biometrics (photo, fingerprints, signature).
