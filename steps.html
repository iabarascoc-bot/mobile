Voter Registration Steps

  1. Check eligibility (18+, Filipino citizen, residency requirements).
  2. Prepare a valid ID (National ID, Passport, etc.).
  3. Go to your local COMELEC office or satellite registration site.
  4. Fill out the registration form.
  5. Have your biometrics taken (photo, fingerprints, signature).
  6. Wait for verification and approval.
  7. Receive your voter registration confirmation.
