Voter Registration Schedule
Region X (Northern Mindanao)

  • January – March: Open registration at COMELEC offices (Mon–Fri, 8AM–5PM)
  • April – June: Satellite registration in malls, barangay halls, and schools (Cagayan de Oro, Iligan, Bukidnon, Ozamiz)
